How to get there?

A ride from a bus in Naga City bus terminal to Tigaon then look for the Police Outpost where the transportation vehicle is located going to Sagnay. Then, ride a “habal-habal” which you can find once you get there in Sagnay, lastly ask the driver to take you in Patitinan.
